Your Patient Data
Stays on Your Device

We believe privacy is a fundamental right in healthcare. Our DICOM viewer uses advanced WebAssembly to process images locally within your browser's memory sandbox.

schedule Effective Date: October 24, 2023

Zero-Upload Architecture

Unlike traditional cloud viewers, our architecture guarantees that sensitive Patient Health Information (PHI) never leaves your machine. We utilize transient memory allocation that is wiped instantly when you close the tab.

memory

Local Processing

All rendering happens on your CPU/GPU using WebAssembly. No file transfer protocols are initiated.

cloud_off

No Cloud Storage

We do not have a backend database for image storage. We cannot see, access, or share your data.

delete_sweep

Ephemeral Session

Data persists only in your browser's RAM. Once the tab is closed, the operating system reclaims the memory.

Detailed Policy Terms

privacy_tip

Data Collection & Anonymity

expand_more

We strictly adhere to a "No-Log" policy regarding PHI. We do not collect names, IDs, birth dates, or any pixel data from the DICOM files you load.

We may collect minimal, anonymous technical telemetry (e.g., browser version, error rates, loading times) to ensure application stability. This data is completely decoupled from any patient information and cannot be used to re-identify any individual.

cookie

Cookie Usage & Local Storage

expand_more

We use Local Storage solely to remember your UI preferences (e.g., dark mode setting, window layout configurations, tool presets).

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes. You can clear your preferences at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

gavel

HIPAA & GDPR Compliance

expand_more

HIPAA (US): Since our application does not transmit PHI to our servers, we do not act as a Business Associate under HIPAA definitions for data storage. However, our client-side encryption and processing standards are designed to support your compliance needs.

GDPR (EU): We act as a Data Processor for the transient session. As no data persists on our systems, "Right to be Forgotten" requests are automatically fulfilled the moment you close the application tab.

hub

Third-Party Subprocessors

expand_more

We utilize minimal third-party services strictly for application delivery:

  • CDN Provider: To deliver the application code (HTML/JS/WASM) to your browser.
  • Analytics (Optional): Aggregated, anonymous usage stats (e.g., Google Analytics 4 in IP-anonymization mode).

None of these providers have access to the medical images loaded into the viewer.

Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your session. You can clear local preferences or contact our Data Protection Officer for any specific concerns.
