security 3. Data Privacy & Local Processing

Our DICOM Viewer operates on a Client-Side Only architecture. This means:

  • No Uploads: Your medical images (DICOM files) are processed exclusively within your web browser's memory. They are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud storage.
  • PHI Protection: Protected Health Information (PHI) contained within the DICOM metadata remains on your local device. We do not collect, store, or transmit patient data.
  • Cookies & Analytics: We may use anonymous cookies solely for performance monitoring and usage statistics, which do not contain any PHI or personal identifiers.

While we design our system to be secure, you are responsible for the security of the device and browser you use to access the Service.
