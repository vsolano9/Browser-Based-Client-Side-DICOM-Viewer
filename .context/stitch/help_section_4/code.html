Frequently Asked Questions

Is my patient data private? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. This viewer operates entirely client-side within your browser. The DICOM images you drag and drop are processed locally on your machine and are never uploaded to any server. Your patient data remains secure on your device.

What file formats are supported? expand_more

We support standard DICOM (.dcm) files, as well as directories containing DICOM series. We also support common medical image formats like NIfTI (.nii, .nii.gz) for research purposes.

How do I save my measurements? expand_more

You can export the current state of the viewer, including all annotations and measurements, by clicking the "Export" button in the top toolbar. You can save as a JSON state file or burn annotations into a PNG image.
